Product Narrative

Step 01

Set the creative perimeter

Every strong asset begins with boundaries. The brand kit gives the model a name, tone, audience, and words to avoid before prompting even begins.

Step 02

Connect the provider directly

Basar AI follows a BYOK model, so the customer controls provider credentials and the system focuses on workflow orchestration rather than usage markup.

Step 03

Generate for where the image will live

Platform presets convert a prompt into output for an actual channel, from LinkedIn banners to Instagram posts and YouTube thumbnails.

Step 04

Keep a clean record

History is part of the product. Teams can inspect what was created, what failed, and which assets belong to each brand without losing operational clarity.
