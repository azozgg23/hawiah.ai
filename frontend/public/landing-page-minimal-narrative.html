Product Narrative

A simple system, intentionally narrow in scope, built to keep multi-brand image generation understandable.

Step 01

Set the creative perimeter

The brand kit gives the model a name, tone, audience, colors, and words to avoid before prompting begins.

Step 02

Connect the provider directly

Basar AI uses a BYOK model, so the customer controls provider credentials while the product focuses on orchestration.

Step 03

Generate for where the image will live

Platform presets turn a prompt into an asset sized for a real channel, from LinkedIn banners to Instagram posts.

Step 04

Keep a clean operational record

Generation history is part of the product, so teams can inspect what shipped, what failed, and what belongs to each brand.
